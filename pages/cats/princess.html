PRINCESS

Princess is a 4 year old tortoiseshell who is very shy but loving.

Princess LOVES pets and cuddles but she HATES people she doesn't know, whenever guests come over she always hides somewhere until the house is cleared.

Princess is very quiet, she doesn't make much noise at all, usually a couple small squeaks here and there when she yawns or stretches, but when she meows you'll definetly hear it.

She'll BEG for pets sometimes even going out of her way to meow at us for them.

Also Smokey bullies her and hits her but princess ends up winning most of the fights.
