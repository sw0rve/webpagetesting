SMOKEY

Smokey is a gray and white cat who is very energetic and playful while also a fat lazy bum who sits on his ass all day and whines at you.

Smokey gets really competitive when you play games with him or when he's playing with Princess, he'll resort to anything to catch the toy on the string, even star-jumping at you.

Smokey trills and meows a lot compared to Princess, he'll make a noise over literally everything.

Mostly food.
